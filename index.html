About Me

I'm Nikini Fernando, a passionate photographer and UI/UX designer who believes in the power of visual storytelling. My journey began with a camera in hand, capturing the world's beauty one frame at a time.

Through photography, I learned to see details others might miss, to find beauty in the ordinary, and to tell stories without words. These skills naturally evolved into my passion for UI/UX design, where I create digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional.
